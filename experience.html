Internship Experience


I once had an experience in internship at Terengganu Library@Perbadanan Perpustakaan Awam Terengganu which known as PPAT. I was placed in three divisions namely the collection management and development division, the library and information services division and finally the library network division. There are a lot of things I learned while being there. For example, in Collection Management and Development division, there are 2 unit which known as Documentation and Technical Unit, and Collection Acquisition unit. There, I learned about how to catalog book and paste spine label at the book according to the association number. While in the library and information services division, I was assigned to take care of every counter in the library such as the main registration counter, children circulation section, adult cyber counter, Terengganu Collection counter, reference desk and also adult circulation desk. In this section I also learned about how to dispose of books either for destruction or donations. Difference with Library Network Section, there I got the opportunity to visit district, town, village and mobile libraries. However if there are no needs and tasks outside the library, I just do the disposal in the space provided.
